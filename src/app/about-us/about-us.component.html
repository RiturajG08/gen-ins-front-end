Our Services

Third Party Insurance

A third-party car insurance is the most basic, and legal form of car insurance policy you will need. As the name suggests, this will only protect you from damages and losses caused to a third-party and won’t cover for your own damages.

Comprehensive Insurance

A comprehensive car insurance is one of the most preferred types of car insurance policies, as it covers for not only third-party liabilities and losses but, also covers for damages caused to you and your own car.
